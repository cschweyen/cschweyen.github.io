About the Artist

Jim Schweyen is a born and raised Montanan. He was raised in East Helena, Montana and has always had a passion for the outdoors. His passion for photography began around 10 years ago and quickly became his hobby. Jim's photography focuses on the great outdoors and wildlife. His goal it to share the beauty that it around his home state, Montana.
